About Me

I’m a licensed civil engineer in California specializing in structural engineering. I’m passionate about problem-solving and collaborating with talented teams of architects, engineers, and builders. I challenge myslef to create structures that bring the design vision to life while balancing cost, constructability, and — above all — safety.

I love having new challenges and believe in being a life long learner with the endless pursuit of self-improvement.

Beyond traditional structural engineering, my master's thesis was on deployable structures and more specifically a novel hinge connection that can be used to create moment capacity within unfoldable 'origami' structures. This design was patented under patent US12098579.

Outside of engineering, I love spending time in nature, whether that's running, hiking, or backpacking. There are very few things that bring me more joy than spending time outdoors with my friends and family. I also have a deep love and passion for music. I play the guitar and electric bass and have recently been teaching my fiance the bass.
